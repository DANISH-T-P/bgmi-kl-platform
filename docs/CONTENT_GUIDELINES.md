# Content Guidelines

## Purpose

This document defines the content strategy, writing standards, communication style, and messaging framework used throughout the BGMI-KL website and community platform.

Every piece of content published under BGMI-KL should reflect our identity, values, and long-term vision.

---

# Brand Voice

BGMI-KL speaks as:

* Professional
* Community-focused
* Supportive
* Competitive
* Inspirational
* Trustworthy

We are not a random gaming group.

We are building Kerala's premier BGMI community platform.

Every word should reinforce that vision.

---

# Tone of Voice

## Primary Tone

Confident but not arrogant.

Professional but not corporate.

Competitive but not toxic.

Friendly but not casual.

---

## Examples

Good:

"Join Kerala's growing BGMI community and compete alongside passionate players."

Bad:

"Join now or miss the biggest BGMI opportunity ever!!!"

---

Good:

"Participate in community tournaments and improve your skills."

Bad:

"Destroy everyone and dominate noobs."

---

# Community Messaging

All communication should encourage:

* Teamwork
* Sportsmanship
* Learning
* Growth
* Community participation

Avoid:

* Toxic language
* Insults
* Harassment
* Elitism
* Rank shaming

---

# Website Messaging Framework

Every major section should communicate:

1. Who we are
2. What we offer
3. Why it matters
4. How users can join

---

# Hero Section Guidelines

Hero sections should contain:

Headline

Supporting Message

Primary CTA

Secondary CTA

---

## Example Structure

Headline:

Kerala's Largest BGMI Community

Supporting Text:

Connect with players, participate in tournaments, build squads, and become part of a growing gaming ecosystem.

Primary CTA:

Join Community

Secondary CTA:

Explore Events

---

# Content Writing Rules

Use:

Short paragraphs

Clear headings

Bullet points

Simple language

Strong calls-to-action

Avoid:

Large blocks of text

Complicated terminology

Clickbait headlines

Excessive emojis

---

# SEO Guidelines

Every page should include:

Unique Title

Meta Description

Structured Headings

Relevant Keywords

Internal Links

Optimized Images

Review

# Final Rule

Every piece of content should answer:

"Does this strengthen the BGMI-KL brand and community?"

If not, revise it.
